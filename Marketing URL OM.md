# OM International School

Welcome to the official OM International School mobile application.

## About

OM International School App is designed to simplify communication and academic management between students, parents, teachers, and school administration.

## Features

* Student Attendance Tracking
* Homework & Assignments
* Exam Results
* Timetable Management
* School Announcements
* Fee Information
* Push Notifications
* Parent-Teacher Communication
