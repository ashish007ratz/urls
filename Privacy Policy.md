# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

OM International School ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of students, parents, teachers, and staff who use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

The application may collect the following information:

* Name
* Email Address
* Phone Number
* Student Information
* Attendance Records
* Academic Performance Data
* Device Information
* Application Usage Data

## How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

* Provide educational and administrative services
* Manage student records
* Display attendance and academic information
* Send notifications and announcements
* Improve application performance and user experience
* Respond to support requests

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared when:

* Required by law
* Necessary for providing school-related services
* Authorized by the user or school administration

## Children's Privacy

The application is intended for use by students, parents, teachers, and school administrators. Information relating to students is handled in accordance with applicable laws and school policies.

## Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services such as:

* Firebase Analytics
* Firebase Cloud Messaging
* Authentication Services
* Cloud Storage Services

These providers may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

## User Rights

Users may request:

* Access to their personal information
* Correction of inaccurate information

Requests should be directed to the contact information below.
