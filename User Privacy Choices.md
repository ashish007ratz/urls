# User Privacy Choices

Last Updated: June 2026

OM International School respects your privacy and provides users with control over their personal information.

## Your Choices

### Access Your Information

You may request access to the personal information associated with your account.

### Correct Your Information

If any information is inaccurate or incomplete, you may request corrections through the school administration.

### Delete Your Information

You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and educational record retention requirements.

### Notification Preferences

You may manage notification settings directly from your device settings.

### Contact Us

For privacy-related requests, please contact:

Email: [support@ominternationalschool.com](mailto:support@ominternationalschool.com)

We will respond to privacy requests within a reasonable timeframe.
